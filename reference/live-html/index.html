About me
Hi,  I'm Sophia.  I love to create things.  My love for art has drawn me to all sorts of mediums: pastels, oil paint, acrylic, watercolor, charcoal, digital, pencil and ink --- you name it. I also found a love for ceramics when studying at Rowan University. 
I graduated from Rowan University in May of 2020, with a BFA in Studio Arts, and a minor in Art History.  While studying at Rowan I concentrated a lot of my studio time in the ceramic studio, where I found a love for working with clay. Other art subjects I love to work with include 2D and 3D design, painting, illustration, jewelry making, candle making, and polymer clay. I am constantly looking to explore the arts and learn new things to create. 
I also love teaching children. Whether that's in large group settings or one-on-one, there is nothing more rewarding than helping kids experience the same joy with art that I experienced when I was their age.
I would love to work with you, whether that means doing a commission, a collaboration, or teaching. Feel free to contact me by filling out the contact form below.
